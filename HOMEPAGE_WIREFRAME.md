## Overall UX Flow & Notes

### Page Flow Strategy
1. **Hero** → Instant understanding of EAP Solutions
2. **EAP Overview** → Quick explanation of what each letter means
3. **Products** → Deep dive into solutions
4. **Benefits** → Why choose Toupto
5. **Case Studies** → Social proof
6. **Services** → Detailed feature list
7. **CTA** → Conversion opportunity
8. **Footer** → Navigation and additional info

### Conversion Funnel
- **Awareness**: Hero, EAP Overview
- **Interest**: Products, Benefits
- **Consideration**: Case Studies, Services
- **Action**: CTA section, CTAs throughout

### Key UX Principles
- **5-second rule**: EAP Solutions clear within 5 seconds (Hero + Section 2)
- **Progressive disclosure**: Overview → Details → Deep dive
- **Multiple CTAs**: Opportunities to convert throughout
- **Trust building**: Stats, testimonials, logos
- **Mobile-first**: All sections responsive
- **Accessibility**: Contrast, focus states, semantic HTML

### CTA Strategy
- **Hero**: Primary conversion point (Explore Solutions)
- **Each section**: Relevant CTAs (Learn More, View Details)
- **Mid-page**: Soft CTAs (View Case Studies)
- **Pre-footer**: Strong CTA (Schedule Demo)
- **Footer**: Navigation, no aggressive CTAs

---

## Mobile Considerations

### Breakpoints
- **Desktop**: Full layout as shown
- **Tablet**: 2-column grids → 1 column, side-by-side CTAs → stacked
- **Mobile**: All cards stack vertically, CTAs full-width

### Mobile-Specific Adjustments
- **Hero**: Larger buttons, reduced text
- **Cards**: Full-width, larger touch targets
- **Navigation**: Hamburger menu
- **CTAs**: Stacked vertically, full-width buttons
- **Footer**: Single column, accordion sections

---

## Performance Goals

- **First Contentful Paint**: < 1.5s
- **Time to Interactive**: < 3s
- **Largest Contentful Paint**: < 2.5s
- **Cumulative Layout Shift**: < 0.1

## Accessibility Checklist

- ✅ Semantic HTML structure
- ✅ Alt text for all images
- ✅ Keyboard navigation
- ✅ Screen reader friendly
- ✅ High contrast ratios (WCAG AA)
- ✅ Focus indicators
- ✅ ARIA labels where needed
